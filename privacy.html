Privacy Policy

Committed to protecting your privacy and personal information

At SHARMORIA, we are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, store, and protect your data when you use our mobile spa services. By booking our services, you consent to the practices described in this policy.

1. Information We Collect

  • Contact Information: Full name, email address, phone number
  • Service Details: Service address, preferred appointment dates and times, selected services
  • Payment Information: Preferred payment method (cash or card)
  • Health Information: Health conditions, allergies, or medical concerns relevant to your treatment (with consent)
  • Communication Records: Records of correspondence between you and SHARMORIA

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Processing and confirming service bookings
  • Communicating appointment details, cancellations, or rescheduling
  • Delivering personalized spa services
  • Maintaining business and legal records
  • Improving service quality and customer experience
  • Sending promotional offers (only with consent)

3. Data Security and Storage

  • All personal data is stored securely and encrypted
  • Access restricted to authorized personnel only
  • Information retained for up to 3 years for business and legal purposes
  • After retention period, data is securely deleted or anonymized

4. Sharing Your Information

  • Service Providers: With our certified therapists providing services to you
  • Legal Obligations: When required by law, court order, or governmental authority
  • Business Transfers: In case of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets

5. Your Rights and Choices

  • Right to Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Right to Correction: Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete information
  • Right to Deletion: Request deletion of personal data (subject to legal obligations)
  • Right to Object: Object to processing of your data for marketing purposes
  • Right to Data Portability: Request transfer of your data to another service provider
  • Right to Withdraw Consent: Withdraw your consent for data processing based on consent

6. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

Our website uses cookies and similar technologies to enhance your browsing experience. You can control cookie preferences through your browser settings.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. Updates will be posted on this page with a revised effective date. Please review regularly.
